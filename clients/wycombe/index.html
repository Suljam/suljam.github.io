Recycling - it's in our name

Wycombe Recycling Ltd is a small family run business offering a range of demolition services. We are the first company in High Wycombe to achieve a Waste Managment License issued by the Environment Agency. This license enables us to recycle much of the collected waste which saves it being disposed of in landfill sites.
